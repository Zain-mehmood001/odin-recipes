Classic Waffles

Description

Waffles are the best to start your day with! This is an easy recipe to make delicious waffles. it takes 10 minutes to prepare, 15 minutes to cook. So that means it takes a total of 25 minutes. This recipe yields 10 to 12 waffles.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 and a half cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cups butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
